Research Topics

FITNESSLAB is an interdisciplinary laboratory between the Department of Engineering (DING) and the Department of Science and Technology at the University of Naples Parthenope. We conduct cutting-edge research in cybersecurity, privacy-preserving computing, and blockchain technologies, with applications spanning computing continuum, eHealth, and industrial control systems.

Our Research Areas

Privacy-Preserving Computing

Research on Trusted Execution Environments (TEE), Homomorphic Encryption (HE), and Federated Learning for secure and private data processing.

AI-Based Security Monitoring

Advanced threat intelligence and security monitoring systems leveraging artificial intelligence and machine learning for information processing.

Blockchain & Web3

Research on blockchain technologies, smart contracts, decentralized systems, and Web3 applications.

Computing Continuum

Security and privacy solutions for edge-to-cloud computing continuum architectures.

eHealth

Secure and privacy-preserving solutions for digital health applications and medical data processing.

Industrial Control Systems

Cybersecurity research for critical infrastructure and industrial control systems (ICS/SCADA).

Research Projects

FITNESSLAB has participated in numerous Italian and European research projects. Our research activities span across multiple funding programs:

European Commission Funded Projects

We have been involved in projects funded under FP7, Horizon 2020, and Horizon Europe programs, focusing on cybersecurity, privacy-preserving technologies, and blockchain applications.

Italian National Projects

Our group participates in PRIN (Progetti di Rilevante Interesse Nazionale) and other national research initiatives, contributing to the advancement of cybersecurity and privacy technologies in Italy.

Spin-off Company

Trust Up

FITNESSLAB has successfully created a spin-off company, Trust Up, which commercializes the research results and innovative technologies developed by our research group. Trust Up focuses on bringing cutting-edge cybersecurity and privacy-preserving solutions to the market, transforming academic research into practical applications for industry and organizations.

Trust Up is an academic spinoff of the University of Naples Parthenope that commercializes research and know-how in the field of secure data processing, specializing in confidential computing based on Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) technologies.
