## About Us

FITNESSLAB is a research laboratory at the University of Naples Parthenope dedicated to advancing cybersecurity, privacy-preserving computing, and blockchain technologies. Our interdisciplinary team works on cutting-edge research problems with applications in computing continuum, eHealth, and industrial control systems.

## Research Topics

### Cybersecurity
- **Privacy-Preserving Computing**: Trusted Execution Environments (TEE), Homomorphic Encryption (HE), Federated Learning
- **Security Monitoring**: AI-based information processing for threat detection and analysis
- **Threat Intelligence**: Advanced systems for threat analysis and prediction

### Application Domains
- **Computing Continuum**: Edge-to-cloud security and privacy solutions
- **eHealth**: Secure and privacy-preserving healthcare systems
- **Industrial Control Systems**: Cybersecurity for critical infrastructure

### Blockchain & Web3
- Smart contract security and analysis
- Decentralized systems research
- Blockchain-based privacy solutions

## Publications

Our research group publishes in top-tier conferences and journals in the fields of cybersecurity, privacy-preserving computing, and blockchain technology. Publication types include:

- **Journal Articles**: High-impact publications in leading international journals
- **Conference Papers**: Peer-reviewed contributions to major international conferences (IEEE, ACM, specialized security venues)
- **Book Chapters**: Contributions to edited volumes on cybersecurity and privacy
- **Workshop Papers**: Early-stage research presented at specialized workshops
- **Technical Reports**: Comprehensive technical documentation
